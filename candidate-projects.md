## Notes on Selection Criteria

**AI-Native Advantage**: Each candidate above benefits significantly from AI in ways that are now technically feasible:
- Document understanding (OCR + LLM extraction)
- Natural language interfaces replacing form-heavy UIs
- Automated classification, tagging, and routing
- Predictive analytics replacing static reports
- Conversational assistants replacing documentation lookup

**Open Source Advantages**: Open source removes pricing barriers, allows self-hosting (data residency), enables community customization, and builds trust in sensitive domains (healthcare, legal, finance).

**Market Timing**: The 2024–2026 window represents a unique opportunity where:
1. AI capabilities have crossed the threshold for practical business use
2. Existing SaaS incumbents have priced out SMBs and mid-market
3. Developer tooling (Next.js, Vercel, etc.) has dramatically reduced build costs
4. Enterprise buyers increasingly evaluate open-core alternatives
