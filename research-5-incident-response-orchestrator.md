# Incident Response Orchestrator

## Existing Products and Software Packages

- **PagerDuty**: Incident response platform with on-call scheduling, escalation, and automation.
- **Opsgenie**: Incident management with alerting, on-call rotation, and integrations.
- **VictorOps (now Splunk On-Call)**: Real-time incident response with collaboration tools.
- **xMatters**: Enterprise incident orchestration with workflow automation.
- **ServiceNow Incident Management**: Integrated with ITSM for incident tracking and response.
- **IBM Resilient**: Incident response orchestration with case management.
- **Atlassian Opsgenie**: Similar to Opsgenie, with Jira integration.

## Relevant Industry Standards or Protocols

- **ITIL (Information Technology Infrastructure Library)**: Framework for IT service management, including incident management processes.
- **NIST SP 800-61**: Computer Security Incident Handling Guide.
- **ISO/IEC 27001**: Information security management standards, including incident response.
- **SRE Practices**: Site Reliability Engineering principles for incident response.

- **Number of Existing Users**: Millions of IT professionals use incident response tools; PagerDuty has over 10,000 customers, Opsgenie thousands. Total market for incident management tools estimated at hundreds of thousands of users.
